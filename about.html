About Affordable Chiropractor Reader

An independent local guide to the cost of chiropractic care in Knoxville, Tennessee, with a focus on Bell Family Chiropractic on Sherlake Lane.

What This Site Is

This site exists to help people in the Knoxville area understand what chiropractic actually costs — new-patient pricing, insurance vs. cash, what drives the total, and how to spot honest pricing — before they call. The information is general and educational and isn't a substitute for an in-person evaluation or an official quote from the office.

About the Practice We Cover

Bell Family Chiropractic is a family office in West Knoxville. The care is hands-on and conservative: adjustments, spinal decompression, soft-tissue work. The goal is simple. Get you back to normal without piling on scans, pills, or surgery. Skip those and your bill stays lower. They see all ages. They take major insurance. They handle car-accident and work-injury cases too. Want a number first? Check Bell Family Chiropractic on Sherlake Lane. The office is located at 111 Sherlake Lane, Suite 101, Knoxville, TN 37922, and the main number is +1 865-383-7730.

How We Maintain Independence

This site is editorial — not the official website of the practice. We summarize publicly available information in plain language and link to the practice for booking and exact pricing. We don't accept advertising and we don't take payment from the practice for coverage. For billing, insurance verification, or any clinical question, contact the office directly.
